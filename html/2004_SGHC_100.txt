This was an appeal against the whole of the decision of the assistant registrar made on 16 September 2003 relating to assessment of damages. The appellant, a 33-year-old Malaysian national, claimed damages and special damages suffered by him in consequence of having sustained a prolapsed central disc between L4 and L5 vertebrae in the course of work on 15 May 1998. The respondent is a company dealing with oil-rig equipment. The appellant was injured when he was lifting and stacking connector forgings used for connecting the ends of metal pipes.
